H.O.M.Y.

COLLECTIVE

The Helping Our Miami Youth (H.O.M.Y.) Collective is a collaborative of over 60 organitations and youth leaders working to prevent and end youth homelessness in Miami-Dade County. The H.O.M.Y. Collective believes that no young person should spend a single night sleeping outdoors simply because they do not have access to safe housing. Our work is driven by the shared goal of ensuring that youth homelessness is rare, brief, and when it does occur, a one-time experience. Through its partner organizations, The H.O.M.Y. Collective provides support and services to youth and young adults between the ages of 13-24 who are experiencing housing instability or in a vulnerable housing situation. Our core areas of support are Stable Housing, Education, Employment, Primary and Behavioral Healthcare, and Permanent Connections.

Miami Homes For All

Miami Homes For All is a registered 501(c)(3) charitable organization whose mission is to promote community collabortation to prevent and end homelessness is South Florida. Miami Homes For All serves as the backbone organizaron of The H.O.M.Y. Collaborative. For more information about Miami Homes For All please visit www.miamihomesforall.org or call 786-469-2060

Miami Dade County Homeless Trust

Founded in 1993, the Miami-Dade County Homeless Trust is a county agency charged with the responsability of implementing the Miami-Dade County Homeless Plan. For more information regarding the Miami-Dade County Homeless Trust, please visit www.homelesstrust.org

Homeless or about to be?

If you are homeless or about to be, or know someone who is, please call the Homeless Helpline at www.homelesstrust.org
